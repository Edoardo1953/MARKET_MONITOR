Installing the App on PC, Tablet and Mobile

Access FOREXcross with one click from any device.

Installation on Computer (PC & Mac)

1
Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge Open the browser on your computer and navigate to the application address.
2
Direct Installation In the address bar (where the URL is), click on the "Install app" icon (a screen with an arrow) on the right.
3
Alternative Method Click the three dots (⋮) top right > Save and share > Install page as app.
4
Desktop Icon The App will open in a dedicated window and an icon will be created on your desktop for quick access.

Installation on Mobile and Tablet

FOR ANDROID (Chrome):
1
Menu Options Tap the three dots (⋮) and select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
FOR iOS / iPHONE / iPAD (Safari):
1
Share Button Tap the Share icon (square with an up arrow) in the bottom bar.
2
Add to Home Screen Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen", then confirm with "Add".
Why install it? The App opens without browser bars, offering a faster, more professional full-screen experience, with the FOREXcross icon always within reach on your desktop or home screen.
